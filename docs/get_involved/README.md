# Getting Involved

The reason you are reading this is great! Probably you came here to see what this is all about and maybe to contribute your piece for a better worldüåç. Contributing to Delft Open Hardware can be super simple and should never feel like a burden. By participating and engaging with the community, you are already contributing. Get involved, it can be something as simple as adding a switch to a piece of hardware or review a snippet of code.

## How to participate:
- Are you interested in doing an open source thesis? Get in contact with us!
- Share a project you are working on.
- Get inspired by what others are doing
- Propose projects to find collaborators.
- Propose ideas to improve our governance and organization.
- Ask for guidance, help or orientation on several topics (Arduino, Python, Sensors,Git, PCB design, CAD modeling)
- Participate in our events and workshops.
